Get To Know Us

Buddify is a peer mentorship program that helps international students settle into life and career in Canada. We connect students with mentors who offer support, career guidance, and real-world insights. Matching is based on shared backgrounds and interests to create meaningful, helpful connections. Grow your network, build confidence, and thrive—socially and professionally—with Buddify.
